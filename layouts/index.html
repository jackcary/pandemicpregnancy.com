Pandemic Pregnancy

Resetting expectations in the time of COVID-19

Updated March 28th, 2020 11:25 AM (EDT)

Congratulations! You're having a baby during a global outbreak and healthcare crisis! Like millions of parents-to-be around the world, you are probably wondering how this situation will impact your pregnancy and childbirth. This website was created to gather information about the current pandemic, as it relates to pregnancy, from healthcare organizations, medical journals, data resources, and news articles. The goal of this project is to help pregnant people and their families reset expectations in the time of COVID-19.
