Pandemic Pregnancy

Resetting expectations in the time of COVID-19

Updated {{ now.Format "January 2, 2006 3:04 PM (MST)" }}

Questions Resources About

Congratulations! You're having a baby during a global virus outbreak and healthcare system crisis! Should you panic? No. Will educating yourself help you make well-informed decisions with your healthcare provider? Yes. Like millions of parents-to-be around the world, you are probably wondering how this situation will impact your pregnancy and childbirth. This website was created to compile questions and information about the current pandemic as it relates to pregnancy from healthcare organizations, medical journals, data resources, and news outlets. The goal of this project is to help pregnant people and their families stay informed and reset expectations in the time of COVID-19.
